Processing and effects run right in your browser - recordings are never uploaded.

How it works: record straight from your mic or upload a take, choose a key and scale (or let it detect the key), then tune the correction strength, snap speed and dry/wet to taste and add a touch of reverb. Compare the original and the processed take side by side and download the result as a WAV. All processing happens locally - your audio never leaves the browser.

Tap, pick an instrument and play one string at a time
Accuracy depends on your mic and background noise. Play one note at a time.

How it works: allow microphone access, choose your instrument (or chromatic) and reference pitch, then play one string at a time. The big note and the needle show the detected pitch and how many cents sharp or flat you are; it turns green when you are in tune. Everything runs locally with the Web Audio API - nothing is recorded or uploaded.
